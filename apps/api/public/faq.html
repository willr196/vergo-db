For staff

Application and onboarding questions.

Apply through the Join VERGO page, choose the roles you can cover, and upload your CV. If there is a fit, the next step is usually an interview.
Relevant experience helps, but attitude, reliability and presentation matter too. VERGO looks for people who can handle service properly.
Applications are reviewed first. Suitable candidates move into interview, right-to-work checks and onboarding steps before any booking is confirmed.
Most shifts are in London and nearby areas. Exact locations, timings and role notes are confirmed in advance of each booking.
Shortlist roles are curated bookings where VERGO reviews all applicants before presenting a shortlist to the client. You apply in exactly the same way as any Standard role. If selected, you earn a £1/hr merit uplift on top of the standard rate for that shift. Strong Shortlist performance is the primary route to Gold status and higher rates on premium bookings.
