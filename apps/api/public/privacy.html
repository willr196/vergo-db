Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This policy explains how VERGO Ltd collects and uses information for event staffing enquiries and applications.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact details such as name, email address, and phone number.
  • Event information provided in quote requests (date, location, roles, and notes).
  • Application details submitted by staff candidates.

2. How We Use Information

  • Respond to enquiries and provide quotes for event staffing.
  • Confirm bookings and communicate event details.
  • Review applications and manage onboarding for staff.

3. Sharing Information

We only share information with service providers who support our operations (such as email or hosting providers). We do not sell personal data.

4. Data Retention

We keep information only as long as needed for operational and legal purposes. You can request removal at any time.

5. Cookies & Analytics

We may use optional analytics to understand how our website is used (for example, which pages are visited). Analytics is only enabled if you choose to accept analytics cookies in the on-site banner. If you reject, analytics will not be loaded.

You can also enable "Do Not Track" in your browser to help limit tracking.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data. To make a request, contact us at wrobb@vergoltd.com.
