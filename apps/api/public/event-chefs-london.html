FAQs

How quickly can you confirm event chefs?

Most enquiries are confirmed same-day where availability allows. For larger teams or specialist requirements, we’ll confirm as soon as the brief is clear.

Do chefs bring their own knives and kit?

Yes, chefs attend event-ready with standard personal kit. If the venue has specific requirements, we include that in the pre-shift briefing.

Can you supply a full kitchen team?

Yes. We can supply chefs plus support roles depending on your menu and service style. Tell us the format and headcount.
