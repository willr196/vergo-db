Pricing FAQs

What's the minimum booking?

We require a minimum of 1 staff member for 4-5 hours. This ensures quality service and makes the booking worthwhile for everyone.

Are there any hidden fees?

None. The rates shown include everything: travel within London, insurance, uniform, and our vetting/coordination service.

Do rates increase for weekends?

Standard rates apply 7 days a week. Peak dates (Bank Holidays, NYE) may have adjusted rates - we'll confirm in your quote.

What's your payment terms?

25% deposit to secure booking, balance due 24 hours before event. Enterprise clients can arrange monthly invoicing.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Free cancellation with 48+ hours notice (full deposit refund). Less than 48 hours may forfeit deposit.

How do volume discounts work?

Total hours across all your bookings count towards volume tiers. Book 50+ hours and automatically unlock Volume rates.
