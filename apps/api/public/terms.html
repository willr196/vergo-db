Terms

Core terms for working with VERGO.

These terms apply to staffing enquiries, confirmed bookings and service delivery carried out by VERGO Ltd.

Last updated January 2025

Short version: bookings are confirmed in writing and both sides need clear information.

If you need detail on a specific booking, contact VERGO directly before confirmation.

Services

Staffing supply only.

VERGO supplies hospitality and event staff including chefs, bar staff, front of house teams, baristas and support roles.

Confirmation

Nothing is booked until confirmed.

Availability is only secured once the booking is agreed and confirmed in writing.

Delivery

Staff are briefed before service.

VERGO handles standards, role notes and event details ahead of the shift wherever reasonably possible.

Client responsibilities

Provide clear and accurate information.

Clients must give correct timing, location, role requirements and safe working conditions for staff.

Invoicing

Handled after delivery unless agreed otherwise.

Invoices are typically based on the confirmed roles and hours delivered, with payment terms shared in booking correspondence.

Liability

Reasonable care, limited responsibility.

VERGO takes reasonable care in supplying services but is not responsible for indirect or consequential loss outside its control.

Questions?

Ask before the booking is locked.

If something needs clarifying, raise it before confirmation so the brief, route and expectations stay aligned.
