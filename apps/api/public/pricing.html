Questions

Pricing questions.

What does the Standard fee cover?

VERGO's matching, vetting, booking coordination and the infrastructure that keeps the staffing process moving cleanly from brief to confirmation.

Why is Standard priced against the agreed wage?

Standard is built for flexible staffing briefs, so the worker rate can adjust by role, seniority and event requirements while VERGO's service fee remains clear and predictable.

What exactly happens on a Shortlist brief?

Applications open for a fixed window, workers apply as normal, VERGO manually reviews the applicant pool and the client receives a refined shortlist to make the final selection from.

Why does Shortlist cost more than Standard?

Shortlist adds a manual curation stage and includes a £1/hr merit uplift for the selected workers. The extra cost reflects stronger matching and helps the brief attract higher-quality applicants.

When should I move up to Gold?

Gold suits senior, lead and high-trust roles where consistency, judgement and guest-facing confidence are central to the event. It is not the default for general staffing cover.

Can I mix solutions on one event?

Yes. Many events use Standard for the wider team, Shortlist for more fit-sensitive roles and Gold for key senior positions. VERGO can price and structure mixed-solution briefs clearly.

What if I want to hire someone directly long-term?

Contact VERGO first. We are always pleased when great people become strong long-term fits, and we can facilitate the conversation and transition. A one-time placement fee from £400 may apply.
