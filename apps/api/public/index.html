Staffing solutions

One staffing system. Three staffing solutions.

VERGO uses the same premium staffing standard across every brief. The solution determines how the brief is fulfilled, how much selection sits with the client, and how the pricing works.

Standard

Flexible, efficient, vetted.

Best for straightforward staffing needs, faster-fill roles and briefs where dependable execution matters most.

  • 01VERGO matches from its screened staffing network and confirms the team directly.
  • 02Designed for general event cover, flexible headcount and operationally simple briefs.
  • 03Commercial model: agreed wage + £3/hr + VAT.
Shortlist

Curated, merit-based, stronger fit.

Used when the brief needs a more selective matching stage and the final fit matters more than the fastest fill.

  • 01Workers apply as normal during a fixed application window.
  • 02VERGO manually reviews every applicant and presents a refined shortlist to the client.
  • 03Commercial model: agreed wage + £4/hr + VAT, including a £1/hr merit uplift for selected workers.
Gold

Premium, managed, senior.

The highest-touch VERGO solution for head chefs, lead bartenders, premium FOH and other key guest-facing roles.

  • 01Matched manually by VERGO for briefs where leadership, trust and consistency matter most.
  • 02Reserved for proven senior staff with the strongest reviews and repeat booking history.
  • 03Commercial model: fixed public rates from £22/hr + VAT, with chef rates at £26/hr + VAT.
