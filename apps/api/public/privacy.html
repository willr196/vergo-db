Privacy

How VERGO handles personal information.

This policy covers enquiry data, booking details and worker applications submitted through VERGO channels.

Last updated January 2025

Short version: we use data to run staffing properly, not to sell it.

If you want access, correction or deletion, contact VERGO directly.

What we collect

Contact and booking information.

Name, email, phone, company, event details and notes you send to us.

Applications

Worker profile details.

Application data, CV files and onboarding information for candidate review.

How we use it

To quote, confirm and deliver.

We use submitted information to respond, manage bookings and handle staffing operations.

Sharing

Only where operations require it.

We may use service providers for hosting, storage or communication. We do not sell personal data.

Retention

Kept only while needed.

We retain information for operational, legal and record-keeping reasons, then remove it when no longer required.

Cookies and analytics

Optional analytics only.

Analytics may be used to understand site usage, but only where the site setup allows it. Browser privacy settings can further limit tracking.

Your rights

Request access, correction or deletion.

Email VERGO if you want us to review the personal information we hold.
