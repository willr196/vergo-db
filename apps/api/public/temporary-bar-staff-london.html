FAQs

Can you provide bar setup support?

Yes. We can supply barbacks and support staff to help maintain stock levels, glassware flow, and a clean bar throughout service.

Do you cover cocktail service?

Yes. Tell us whether you have a fixed cocktail menu, any spec sheets, and expected volume so we can match the right bartenders.

How many bartenders do I need?

Team size depends on guest count and complexity. As a starting point, cocktail service typically needs more staff than basic bar service. Share your guest count and format and we’ll recommend a setup.
