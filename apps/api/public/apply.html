Our Onboarding Process

1

Application Review

We review your application and experience to see if there's a fit. Experience is preferred but not mandatory.

2

Interview

If suitable, we'll arrange a brief interview to understand your experience and availability.

3

Right-to-Work Check

We verify your right to work in the UK as part of our onboarding requirements.

4

Training (if required)

If experience is limited, we provide small training sessions to ensure you meet our standards.

5

Standards Briefing

Before your first booking, you'll receive a briefing on our presentation and service standards.
